FAQ

Common
Questions.

Everything you need to know before getting started.

Will my website be mobile-friendly and SEO-optimised?
Absolutely. Every website we build is fully responsive — meaning it looks and works perfectly on phones, tablets, and desktops. SEO foundations are built in from the start — page titles, meta descriptions, site structure, and speed optimisation are all included as standard.
Do you offer ongoing support after the website is launched?
Yes. We provide maintenance, updates, and support after launch. We're a long-term partner, not a one-time vendor. If something needs updating — new menu, new team member, new service — we're here to handle it.
How do you ensure my website aligns with my brand?
We collaborate closely with you at every stage. Before we build anything, we get a thorough understanding of your brand — your colours, your tone, your audience, your goals. Everything we design is reviewed and approved by you before it goes live.
How long does it take to build a website?
Typically 1–3 weeks depending on the complexity of the site and how quickly content and feedback is provided. We move fast — but we never cut corners. You'll always have a clear timeline from day one.
Do I need to provide content and images?
We can work with whatever you have. If you have photos and content ready, we'll use them. If not, we can help you structure the content and guide you on what's needed. We also offer basic copywriting as part of our broader service packages.
